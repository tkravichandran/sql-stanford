Description

As a data scientist/engineer you facilitate data-driven decision-making by making data available for analysis in an accessible format. You do this by leveraging your expertise and skills in computer science, applications and statistics. You build a detailed understanding of the data, and use this in collaboration with other teams to find valuable insights from and ensure the validity of the data managed by GRESB. You work in a team with other data scientists, and report to the Lead Data Scientist. As a data scientist/engineer, your tasks include:

  • ETL: extracting, cleaning, transforming, and analyzing data for internal and external stakeholders
  • Contributing to development of internal R packages, performing code reviews, and writing documentation
  • Improving data validation/quality processes
  • Collaborating with software engineers to implement data science algorithms in production code and provide advice on data structures
  • Collaborating with non-technical team members, acting as the technical expert for product development


The IT & Analytics Team

As a data scientist/engineer, you will be part of the larger IT & Analytics team, which includes product owners and software engineers in addition to data scientists/engineers. Our team embraces Agile methodologies and values open communication and a collaborative spirit. We are looking for someone who has a growth mindset and is excited to learn, grow, and adapt over time.

For this position we are looking for the right candidate to complement our team, and are flexible to experience. Both junior and medior candidates will be considered.


Tech Stack

The tech stack across the full IT & Analytics team includes: R, PostgreSQL, Dash, Shiny, Ruby on Rails, Go, StimulusJS and React. We use Github to manage our code repository and AWS to host our software.

Requirements

To be eligible for this position you need to have 2 years of relevant work experience.

Experience with the following is a must:

  • R, Python, Julia or any other data science programming language
  • SQL
  • Git

Experience with the following is a plus:

  • Working with Github or similar to collaborate on shared code
  • Developing and deploying applications
  • Cloud technologies such as AWS
  • Docker and web API framework

About GRESB

GRESB was launched ten years ago by a group of large pension funds who wanted to have a better engagement with their managers on sustainability issues. In the intervening years, we have grown to become the leading Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) benchmark for real estate and infrastructure investments across the world.

Our ESG data and benchmarks now cover over USD 5.3 trillion in real estate and infrastructure value and are used by more than 100 institutional investors to make decisions that lead to a more sustainable world.

We have an engaged community of asset managers who are proud to participate in our annual benchmarking process and a group of inspirational industry partners and ESG service providers who amplify our reach.

The GRESB team comes from diverse backgrounds with experience spanning climate, sustainability, data science, software development, real estate, infrastructure, and the capital markets. Were passionate about tackling the sustainability challenge by providing the insights that will power the markets of the future and enable investors to allocate the worlds capital in a more responsible way.

We are an energetic group of 35 people, covering many different nationalities, located in Amsterdam, with satellite offices in Washington DC and Sydney. We care deeply about creating a positive and inclusive working environment where everyone feels comfortable contributing their ideas. In the pre-COVID 19 period we had shared lunches, regular happy hours and team retreats.
